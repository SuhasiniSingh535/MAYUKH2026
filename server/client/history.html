2025

Pirates in the Silicon Valley

The boldest edition yet, "Pirates in the Silicon Valley" in 2025 celebrated fearless thinkers who dared to disrupt. The campus turned into a sea of innovation — hackathons themed around digital rebellion, startup simulations, and design battles. Participants took inspiration from tech mavericks like Steve Jobs, Grace Hopper, and Ada Lovelace.

2024

Ved se Vigyaan

The 2024 theme, "Ved se Vigyaan," bridged India's ancient wisdom with modern scientific thought. Students explored the roots of algorithms in Vedic mathematics, the science of sound in Sanskrit phonetics, and traditional astronomical systems.

2023

Women in STEM

The journey of Mayukh began with a theme that celebrated the brilliance and resilience of women in technology. "Women in STEM" highlighted the power of inclusivity and equal opportunity in innovation.
