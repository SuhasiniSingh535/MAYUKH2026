About Us

Mayukh 2026

Mayukh is the grand annual technical fest of Banasthali Vidyapith – a convergence of innovation, creativity, and technology. It brings together brilliant minds from across disciplines to collaborate, compete, and redefine possibilities through hackathons, tech events, and project showcases. Inspired by the brilliance of light, *Mayukh 2026* shines as a celebration of intellect, teamwork, and futuristic vision – illuminating the path toward a smarter tomorrow.
